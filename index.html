Engineering a Cost-Effective Irrigation System for Greenhouse Operations

In this project, I optimized a greenhouse irrigation system using an existing pump near Lake Erie. The goal was to efficiently deliver water to a storage tank while minimizing costs over 10 years. I recommended using 2" HDPE tubing along a specific route, estimating an annual electricity cost of $531.8 and a total 10-year cost of $16,694. Assumptions included consistent material properties and adherence to engineering equations. The recommended design proved the most cost-effective and efficient among alternatives considered.

Finite Element Analysis & Design Optimization of Space Truss

In this project, we designed and optimized a cantilevered space truss made of 6061-T6 aluminum alloy for lunar scientific experiments. The truss needed to support a specified mass at the far end under lunar gravity conditions. My partner and I successfully reduced the truss's mass from 2.89 kg to 0.89 kg, ensuring no buckling or node removal. Our design was recognized among the top five best truss designs.

The Benefits of Project-Based Learning in Engineering

In this white paper, I focused on the challenges that engineering students face when it comes to balancing coursework and hands-on projects. The paper outlines how this imbalance has led to students feeling disillusioned and uninspired about their chosen field of study. As a solution, engineering students should have more opportunities to engage in project-based learning, which would not only help them develop their skills but also increase their passion for engineering.

Mass Shootings: Working to Frame the Problem by Evaluating Proposed Solutions

In this project, we utilized engineering principles to frame the problem of mass shootings and evaluate proposed solutions. We applied reverse engineering and the engineering design process to gain a deeper understanding of the issue and identify effective mitigation strategies. By comparing and contrasting existing active shooter technologies and procedures, we recognized patterns and defined parameters for successful solutions. Our goal was to provide a comprehensive overview of current solutions and inspire others to tackle this complex problem with evidence-based approaches.
